Our Goal! Motivations & Inspirations

Our mission is to prevent any future collision with sets of warning indicators built into Google's map! We want to give thanks to Google's API for maps and also give thanks to IBM's API for audio output. The idea of the project was inspired by the collection of data from NYC OpenData. Thus the creation of Vision [0] was born, past collections of data can provide a statistical hotspot where collision usually occurs on the road. From road bicycles to automobile, we can establish a safer route to user's destination. MORE CAUTION! MEAN A LOT SAFER!
